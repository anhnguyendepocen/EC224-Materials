The estimate of the standard error of the regression is 
\begin{equation}
\sqrt{\frac{\frac{1}{n-2} \sum_{1=1}^n (y_i - \hat{y})^2}{\sum_{i = 1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2}}
\end{equation}
